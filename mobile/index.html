About Us

At OpenGenetics, we embrace the belief that everyone deserves equal access to information, especially when that information pertains to reproductive liberty. We provide an analysis of genetic risk by sorting embryos based on their predicted income and cognitive ability, and we offer a haven of understanding and inclusiveness for families from all walks of life.

Our Services

Our first-of-its-kind service is designed to assist families in making informed decisions on their journey towards parenthood. Using cutting-edge machine learning and genetic analysis, we analyze the DNA of embryos and present the results in an easy-to-understand document, which clearly emphasizes the uncertainty inherent to genetic testing.
